🌈 HARMONIC SERIES SYNTHESIZER · v4.1

88-key sinusoidal synth · pitch-accurate harmonic placement · position-locked slide-play: in Harmonic mode, sliding the cursor across the keyboard plays each harmonic exactly when the cursor is over its corresponding piano key.

Slide-Play interaction conceived and developed by Leonardo Fuks for Sérgio Basbaum — research project "How Harmonic are Jew's Harps?", UFRJ.

Click and drag across the keyboard. In Harmonic mode, the cursor's position on the keyboard locks the harmonic to the key it is over: H1 sounds while the cursor is over C1, H2 over C2, H3 over G2, H4 over C3, H5 over E3, etc. In Chromatic mode it steps through semitones; in Free Sinus it glides continuously with magnetic harmonic snap.
